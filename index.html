Roles such as the Student Representative (General Affairs) managing external communications and project permissions which includes various departments.

STUDENT REPRESENTATIVE
(General Affairs)

Appointed by the Senior Student Advisor, he/she is the de-facto head of the student-led initiative and shall serve as the agent of correspondence between external organizations and Build-NEO, as well as the person of contact for those (non-members) attempting to work in collaboration with active members on student projects.

ELECTRONICS

Possesses inventory list of all electronics held by Build-NEO and their current state of operation. Appointed by Senior Student Advisor and his/her team. Responsible for supervising a junior electronics department as well as actively acquiring, testing, developing newer custom equipment for projects being undertaken by Build-NEO.

CODING

Responsible for maintenance, performance reports, overseeing the debugging and testing process of all software applications/programs deployed/incorporated in student projects developed by Build-NEO.

CAD DRAFTING/MODELLING

Responsible for designing custom parts/structures for upgrades of existing and future projects. He/she supervises the drafting projects created by the junior team and supervises them. Parts to be put forth for machining and additive manufacturing via 3D printing are overseen by him/her.

DEPARTMENTS

FABRICATION

Responsible for material selection and acquisition of structural components of project based on specific requirements. Oversees working of junior team in the fabrication and preparation of surfaces of materials to be used for construction of prototypes and physical models.

ADDITIVE MANUFACTURING

Responsible for maintenance of custom-designed 3D printed sub-units and their incorporation into existing projects for upgrades or construction of newer projects. Maintenance and report preparation of status of incorporation of designs by CAD Drafting/Modelling department.

TECHNICAL DOCUMENTATION

Oversees authenticity, clarity of intent and transparency of all forms of documentation issued by Build-NEO, he/she shall oversee, proof-reading, plagiarism checks, AI generated content detection for the same.

SOCIAL MEDIA MANAGEMENT

Responsible for maintenance of social media handles and overseeing production of content aimed at promoting the initiative and increasing its public outreach. Actively work towards establishing contacts and expand network to other similar student led organizations in India.

About Us

BuildNEO is an independent student-led research and development initiative, started by the passionate and driven students of Indian Maritime University, Visakhapatnam. We aim to be a platform for practical skill development and project-based learning for aspiring naval architects.
